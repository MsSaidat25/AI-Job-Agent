AI Job Agent

An AI-powered career strategist that finds jobs, tailors resumes, applies autonomously, and provides location-aware salary intelligence, all while protecting user privacy.

PRD v2.0 March 15, 2026 AVIEN SOLUTIONS

Overview

Why this product exists and who it serves.

Problem Statement

Job seekers spend hours daily copying the same resume into different portals, get ghosted on the majority of applications, lack market salary data, and have no systematic way to track progress or learn from rejections. Career changers and international seekers face additional challenges: they don't know which skills transfer, what salary to expect in different markets, or how to tailor applications for regional norms.

Target Users

  • Mid-level professionals seeking remote/international roles
  • Senior engineers exploring relocation opportunities
  • Career changers pivoting to new fields
  • Fresh graduates entering the job market

The User Journey

From first visit to "9 jobs applied, 1 needs your tap." Click any step to expand.

0 Signup & Onboarding

Three ways in, nobody left behind:

  • "I have a resume" → Upload it (Step 1)
  • "I don't have a resume" → The agent walks you through questions about your education, projects, and skills. Builds your first resume from the conversation.
  • "I just want to look around" → Jump straight to the job feed with a bare-bones profile. Fill it in before applying.
Connect your email (Gmail or Outlook, via OAuth) during signup. This powers autonomous apply and response tracking. Use your main address or set up a +jobs alias for separation.
Platform: Mobile-first web app, built as a PWA. Works on phone, tablet, or laptop. No app store, no install.
1 Upload Resume & Smart Parse

Hand it a PDF, Word doc, or even a photo of a printed resume. The AI pulls out everything:

  • Contact info, location, languages
  • Work history with dates, titles, and accomplishments
  • Skills, mapped to O*NET and ESCO taxonomies
  • Education and certifications
Everything's pre-filled but editable. Fields the parser isn't sure about get flagged with confidence indicators. Your profile sticks around across sessions and devices.
Parsing stack: Claude Vision for the hard stuff (scanned pages, multi-column layouts, non-English resumes). Gemini Flash for clean text extraction.
2 Know Your Worth (Location-Based Salary Calibration)

Before applying anywhere, the agent compares the user's resume against real market data for their selected location(s):

  • Market arbitrage: "Your skills pull $95K remote from Lagos, $140K in Berlin, $180K in San Francisco"
  • Level calibration: "You qualify for Senior. You're searching Mid-level. Aim higher."
  • Salary range: Floor, target, and stretch numbers, broken out by user's chosen region(s)
  • Country opportunities: "The Netherlands has strong demand for your stack, good visa sponsorship, and pays 20% more"
Location is the anchor. All salary recommendations, job matches, and market insights are filtered and calibrated to the location(s) the applicant selects. Users can add multiple target locations to compare.
Data sources: BLS OEWS API (occupation-level wages by metro area), H-1B LCA disclosure data (company-specific salaries), salary ranges parsed from live job postings, plus LLM analysis layered on top. Every number shows its source and last-updated date.
3 Skill Gap Intelligence

The agent analyzes live job postings for target roles in the user's chosen location:

  • Must-have gaps: "85% of Senior Data Engineer roles in Berlin ask for Spark. You don't have it."
  • Nice-to-have gaps: "Kubernetes shows up in 40% of postings but almost never as a hard requirement."
  • Hidden strengths: "Only 12% of candidates in Toronto have Airflow experience. You do. Lead with that."
  • Upskill ROI: "Getting an AWS cert would bump your match rate by 30% and move you up ~$15K in SF. Takes ~6 weeks."
  • Learning paths: Specific courses, certs, and time estimates
Every recommendation comes with a dollar figure tied to the user's location: "Learning X is worth $Y more per year in [location]."
4 Job Discovery (All Sources, One Feed)

Jobs from multiple sources, deduplicated into one feed:

  • Aggregator APIs: Adzuna, JSearch, Jooble, Reed, Arbeitnow, Remotive, The Muse
  • Paste a URL: Found something on LinkedIn? Paste the link. The agent grabs the listing, scores it.
  • Company watchlist: Pick companies; the agent monitors their careers pages
  • Same job on 3 boards? You see it once with all sources listed
  • Every listing gets a 0-100 score with explanation + salary market context for your location
"You're only looking at Data Analyst roles, but your profile is a really good fit for Analytics Engineer too. Want me to pull those in?"
5 Per-Job Tailored Documents

Every application gets its own resume. Not one generic version sent to 50 companies:

  • Keywords pulled from the job description for ATS optimization
  • Achievements reordered and reframed for the specific role
  • Cover letter with a real hook about the company
  • Export as PDF, DOCX, or Markdown
  • Tailoring notes show exactly what changed and why
  • Nothing gets fabricated. The agent only uses verified profile data.
Over time, the agent tracks which versions work: "Your technical-tone resumes get twice as many callbacks for backend roles."

7 Response Tracking & Pipeline
  • Every application tracked: submitted → under review → interview → offer/rejected
  • Email monitoring via OAuth: Agent reads employer replies, classifies them (rejection, interview invite, offer), and auto-updates the pipeline
  • Interview detected? "You've got an interview at Stripe, Thursday 2pm. Want me to add it to your calendar? Here's a prep doc."
  • Ghosted for 7 days? Agent drafts a follow-up email for your review
  • All employer feedback logged and analyzed across full history
How email works: Gmail API with gmail.readonly and gmail.labels scopes. Agent sets up a "Job Applications" label and only watches that label. Never touches personal inbox. Revocable anytime.
8 AI Career Advisor

Not just a form filler, but a strategic advisor that improves with use:

  • Weekly brief: "You applied to 34 jobs this week. Got 8 callbacks (24%, up from 15%). Fintech is your strongest sector. Technical-tone resumes outperform professional ones 2:1."
  • Pivot suggestions: "You keep getting turned down for Staff roles. Senior is a stronger fit right now. Want to adjust targets?"
  • Salary negotiation: "They offered $130K. For your profile in [your location], $145K is more in line. Here's a counter script with the numbers to back it up."
  • Interview prep: Mock interviews tailored to the role, common questions for the company, research brief
  • Honest assessment: "This one's a stretch. Here's what you'd need to close the gap. But also, here's a closer match you missed."
The more you use it, the better it gets. It learns which roles you skip, which companies excite you, and what feedback you're getting. Recommendations sharpen over time.

Salary & Market Data Strategy

All salary recommendations are anchored to the applicant's selected location(s). Every number shows its source.

Data Sources (Tiered by Feasibility)

BLS OEWS API

Occupation-level median/mean wages by metro area & industry. Government data, no restrictions on AI use.

Coverage: All US occupations · Update: Annual

FREE · 500 queries/day

H-1B LCA Disclosure Data

Company-specific salaries: employer name, job title, offered wage, worksite location. Millions of records. Especially strong for tech.

Coverage: All H-1B sponsors · Update: Quarterly bulk download

FREE · DOL bulk download

Job Posting Salary Ranges

Parsed from job listings the agent already pulls. Pay transparency laws (NY, CA, CO, WA) mean more postings include ranges.

Coverage: Growing with legislation · Update: Real-time

FREE · extracted from job feeds

Levels.fyi API

Best tech compensation data (base + stock + bonus by company/level). Explicitly supports AI agents and offers structured data for LLMs.

Coverage: Tech roles · Access: Contact sales

ENTERPRISE · pricing on request

HR-reported compensation data with AI integration documentation. OAuth 2.0 REST API.

Coverage: All industries · Access: partnerships@salary.com

Security

  • Encryption at rest: AES-256-GCM for all PII fields
  • Key derivation: PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256, 390K iterations
  • LLM data isolation: Only sanitized fields sent to LLM
  • Bias mitigation: Protected attributes stripped before scoring
  • OAuth scopes: Gmail/Outlook with minimal readonly + labels
  • Resume storage: Encrypted at rest, deleted after parsing unless user opts to keep
  • API security: CORS, session isolation, structured errors

Scalability

  • Phase 1: Single-user SQLite, in-memory sessions
  • Phase 2: PostgreSQL via Alembic, connection pooling, async DB
  • Phase 3: Multi-tenant auth, rate limiting, Redis sessions

Reliability

  • Health check endpoint always available
  • Graceful error handling via AppError classes
  • with_retry decorator for external service calls
  • Structured error format: { error: { code, message } }
  • Salary data fallback chain: BLS → H-1B → job postings → LLM estimates (always labeled)

Risk Register

What could go wrong, how bad it would be, and how we handle it.

CRITICAL

Platform Account Bans

Automating LinkedIn/Indeed submissions gets users permanently banned. 461+ tools blacklisted.

Mitigation: Only auto-submit through safe channels. LinkedIn/Indeed apps queued for user's own tap. Zero ban risk by design.
CRITICAL

Legal Liability (ToS / Scraping)

hiQ v. LinkedIn ended with $500K judgment and forced data destruction.

Mitigation: All job data from legitimate APIs or single-URL parsing user initiates. No scraping.
HIGH

AI Hallucinating Credentials

36% of hiring managers reject AI-generated applications. Invented credentials destroy interviews.

Mitigation: Writer agent only uses verified profile data. Every doc shows a diff. Hard guardrails in system prompts.
HIGH

Stale Salary Data

If the AI guesses at salaries, users ask for too much or too little.

Mitigation: Multi-source strategy (BLS + H-1B + job postings). Every number shows source and last-updated date. Monthly refresh.
HIGH

Aggregator APIs Deprecate

Indeed already killed their API. Any aggregator could do the same.

Mitigation: Pull from multiple sources; never depend on one. User-pasted URLs work as fallback.
MEDIUM

Email OAuth Verification Delay

Google's OAuth review takes 4-6 weeks. Blocks autonomous apply if not started early.

Mitigation: Start verification early. Use minimal scopes. Tool works without email; users update statuses manually.
MEDIUM

User Churn After Placement

Most searches take 3-6 months. Users cancel after landing a job.

Mitigation: "Career growth mode" with salary benchmarking, skill tracking, passive monitoring. Annual pricing + referral program.
MEDIUM

GDPR / Privacy Regulations

Resumes are personal data. EU can fine up to 4% of revenue.

Mitigation: AES-256-GCM encryption. Self-hosted option. EU data residency. One-click deletion. Full DSAR compliance.
