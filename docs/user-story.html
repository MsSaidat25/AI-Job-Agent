Who We Serve

Meet the people we are building for

Each persona represents thousands of real job seekers (and one hiring manager) whose daily frustrations shaped every feature in this product.

A
Amara, The Remote Worker
Mid-Level Data Analyst · Lagos, Nigeria
Goal: Land a remote EU/US role without spending 3+ hours per day on job portals.
Pain: Resume copy-paste fatigue. 90% ghost rate. No salary benchmarking for cross-border roles.
I spend my evenings reformatting the same resume for every single portal. Then I never hear back.
M
Marcus, The Underpaid Senior
Senior DevOps Engineer · Berlin, Germany
Goal: Understand his true market value and explore US relocation opportunities.
Pain: Zero salary visibility. Previous automation tools got his LinkedIn account flagged.
I tried one of those auto-apply tools. Two weeks later, my LinkedIn was restricted. Never again.
P
Priya, The Career Changer
Marketing Manager → Product Management
Goal: Identify which of her skills transfer and build a plan to close the gaps.
Pain: Does not know which skills transfer. Overwhelmed by job requirements that list 20 "must-haves."
Every PM job listing asks for SQL, A/B testing, and roadmapping. I do not even know where to start.
D
David, The Fresh Graduate
CS Graduate · Toronto, Canada
Goal: Land his first tech job without insider connections or paid tools.
Pain: Caught in the "need experience to get experience" trap. Cannot afford $99/mo subscription tools.
I graduated four months ago. Every "entry level" posting wants 3 years of experience.
S
Sarah, The Hiring Manager Coming Soon
Engineering Manager · Employer Portal Persona
Goal: Find pre-qualified candidates without sifting through 500 unqualified applications.
Pain: Job boards send volume, not quality. Wants AI-matched candidates, not keyword spam.
I posted one role and got 487 applications. Maybe 12 were actually qualified. That is not sustainable.
Step by Step

User Journeys

Walk through the product as each persona would experience it, from first sign-up to daily use.

A

Amara's First-Time Setup

From zero to fully configured profile in under 10 minutes.

1

Sign Up

Amara signs up with email or Google via Supabase Auth. No credit card required.

2

Upload Resume

She uploads her PDF resume. The AI parses it into a structured profile, showing confidence scores for each field.

AI Confidence: Name 99% · Skills 94% · Experience 91% · Education 97%
3

Review & Correct

She reviews the parsed fields. A few items flagged as low-confidence are easy to correct inline.

4

Set Preferences

Desired roles, locations (remote EU/US), salary range, and remote-only preference.

5

Privacy Dashboard

The privacy panel shows exactly what is shared and what is encrypted.

"Your data is encrypted. Only skills and location are shared with AI."
M

Marcus's Daily Job Search

From dashboard to downloaded application package in 5 minutes.

1

Dashboard Overview

Marcus opens his dashboard and sees KPI cards: total applications, response rate, interview rate.

2

AI-Powered Search

He clicks "Find Jobs." The AI searches across JSearch, Adzuna, and Greenhouse/Lever APIs simultaneously.

3

Smart Results

Each result shows a match score, salary vs. market comparison, and company intelligence summary.

Match: 92% · Salary: EUR 85k (Market avg: EUR 78k) · Company: Series C, 200 employees
4

Tailored Preview

He clicks a listing and sees a tailored resume preview with ATS compatibility score.

5

Download Package

One click downloads a PDF package: tailored resume, cover letter, and company cheat sheet.

6

Apply Safely

Marcus applies on the actual job site himself. No browser automation, no account risk.

Zero automation on job platforms. His accounts stay safe.
P

Priya's Career Dreaming

From "can I even do this?" to a concrete 12-month plan.

1

Open Career Dreamer

Priya enters her target: "Product Manager" in London with a 12-month timeline.

2

AI Gap Analysis

The AI maps her existing skills to the target role and returns a detailed breakdown.

Skill overlap: 65% · Missing: SQL, A/B testing, roadmapping · Feasibility: 72/100
3

Salary Comparison

Current salary ($75k Marketing Manager) vs. target ($95k Product Manager). Clear upside.

4

Learning Plan

A week-by-week plan: free courses, certifications, portfolio projects. Prioritized by impact.

5

Track Progress

Priya saves the dream and revisits it monthly to track progress and adjust the plan.

D

David's First Job Hunt

From no resume and no connections to a structured daily job search.

1

Free Tier Sign-Up

David signs up on the free tier. No credit card, no trial expiration on core features.

2

Build Resume from Scratch

He has no resume yet. The AI helps him build one from his coursework, projects, and hackathon experience.

AI turns "built a React weather app" into a professional achievement bullet.
3

Daily Matches

The AI identifies 5 high-match entry-level roles per day, filtered for "truly entry-level" (no 3-year experience traps).

4

Tailored Applications

For each role, a tailored resume is generated with ATS scoring. He learns what works.

5

Track What Works

David tracks every application. Over time, he sees which resume styles and job types get responses.

Week 3: "Roles with company size <50 have 3x response rate for you."
S

Sarah's Employer Posting Coming Soon

From job posting to qualified, opted-in candidates.

1

Employer Sign-Up

Sarah signs up on the employer portal, a separate experience from the job seeker side.

2

Post a Role

She posts a job with requirements, culture notes, and salary range.

3

AI Matching

The AI matches her posting against anonymized candidate profiles. No names, no demographics. Just skills.

12 candidates matched at 80%+ · Average match score: 87%
4

Review Anonymized Profiles

Sarah sees match scores and anonymized skill profiles. She shortlists 5 candidates.

5

Candidate Opt-In

Shortlisted candidates receive a notification. They choose whether to reveal their identity and connect.

Candidates control their own data. They opt in, not the other way around.
Pain → Solution

Every feature exists because something hurts

What job seekers struggle with today, how competitors handle it, and why our approach is fundamentally different.

The Pain

"I applied to 500 jobs and got 2 responses."

Competitors

LazyApply: blast even more applications. Results in 0.1% response rates.

Our Approach

Quality matching: fewer, targeted applications with higher response rates.

The Pain

"I do not know if my resume will pass ATS."

Competitors

Jobscan: scan-only, no fixes. Costs $50/mo separately.

Our Approach

ATS scoring and auto-fix built into resume generation. Included, not upsold.

The Pain

"These tools send my personal data to their servers."

Competitors

All SaaS competitors store your data on their cloud. You have no control.

Our Approach

AES-256-GCM encryption, EU hosting, PII scrubbing, and a transparency dashboard.

The Pain

"My LinkedIn got flagged for using automation."

Competitors

AIHawk and LazyApply use browser automation. Known account risk.

Our Approach

No browser automation, ever. API-only architecture. Zero account risk.

The Pain

"I cannot afford $99/month while job hunting."

Competitors

LazyApply charges $99-249/mo. Jobscan is $50/mo. Adds up fast.

Our Approach

Free tier with real, usable features. Pro tier at $19/mo for power users.

The Pain

"I do not know what I am worth in another country."

Competitors

Nobody does cross-border salary benchmarking well. Data is scattered.

Our Approach

BLS + Eurostat salary benchmarks with multi-currency comparison built in.

The Pain

"I do not know if my skills transfer to a new role."

Competitors

Nobody offers this. Career changers are on their own.

Our Approach

Career Dreamer: gap analysis, feasibility scoring, and a week-by-week learning timeline.
