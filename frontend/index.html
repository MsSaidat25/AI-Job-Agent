How It Works

One platform. Every step of your job search.

Upload your resume or build a profile. JobPath AI handles the rest.

search

Smart Job Search

Search thousands of real listings from JSearch and Adzuna. Every result is scored 0 to 100 against your skills and experience so you focus on roles where you have the best chance.

description

Tailored Documents

Generate resumes and cover letters matched to each job description. Choose professional, creative, or technical tone. Export as PDF or DOCX. ATS keyword scoring built in.

analytics

Application Tracking

Track every application from draft to offer. Kanban board view, response rate analytics, and AI-generated insights that show you what is working and what to adjust.

shield

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. PII is encrypted at rest with AES-256. Demographic attributes are stripped before AI processing to prevent bias. No data is sold or shared.
